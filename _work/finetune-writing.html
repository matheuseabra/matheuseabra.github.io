Changing how writing assessments are evalutated, at scale


FineTune Writing is a cloud-based solution for benchmark and formative writing assessments in grades 6-12 that seamlessly integrates high-quality content, automated feedback, hybrid (A.I./human) rubric-based evaluation, and detailed reporting to inform instruction, tighten the feedback loop, and measure student performance over time.

My role within the team


I was responsible for the full cycle of Development, as well as Deployment and maintenance of the codebase in production.
