Improving the invoicing experience of franchisees


Mooz Boleto is a product of Mooz's product line, it helps franchises to manage their invoicing experience with resellers.

My role within the team


Clients needed to design and develop the experience for their new product. After some kickstart meetings, the client and Product Owner decided on doing the redesign of the UI Layout.

I was responsible for the developing the client-side architecture of the app as well as deploying it to the cloud. After initial Product Design, the Development cycle mainly focused on building components that could be reused as the brand continues to grow.
