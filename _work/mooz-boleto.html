Improving invoicing experience of franchises


Mooz Boleto is a product of Mooz's product line, it helps franchises to manage their invoicing experience with resellers. Clients needed someone to design the experience for their product. After some kickstart meetings, clients and Product Owner decided that a clean and minimal web UI would be the choice for their frontend.

After Product Design, the Development cycle mainly focused on building components that could be reused as the brand continues to grow.
